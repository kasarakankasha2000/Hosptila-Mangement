CRITICAL CARE UNIT

The Critical Care Unit consists of state of the art 30 bedded Intensive Care Complex with full time highly trained and experienced Intensivist. It is one of its kind set up in Marathwada region An intensive care unit (ICU), also known as an intensive therapy unit or intensive treatment unit (ITU) or critical care unit (CCU), is a special department of a hospital or health care facility that provides intensive treatment medicine. Intensive care units cater to patients with severe or life-threatening illnesses and injuries, which require constant care, close supervision from life support equipment and medication in order to ensure normal bodily functions. They are staffed by highly trained physicians, nurses and respiratory therapists who specialize in caring for critically ill patients.

Services


⊙ Bedside invasive monitoring in the form of central / arterial lines in critically ill patients to measure CRP / ABP.

⊙ Insertion of Swanganz Catheters / IABP's for suitable patients to measure cardiac output.

⊙ Usage of advanced ventilators for invasive and non invasive ventilation with optimal use of new modes.

⊙ Management of post CABG / Value replacement and major post operative cases.

⊙ Bedside Haemo- Dialysis facility.
